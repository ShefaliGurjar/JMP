Workshops

Data Analysis Workshop

Workshops are intensive, hands-on sessions designed to impart practical skills and knowledge in a specific area. This Data Analysis Workshop will cover advanced techniques, including regression analysis, machine learning algorithms, and data visualization, with real-world case studies. Participants will gain proficiency in using industry-standard tools and methodologies. Past workshops have featured guest speakers from leading tech companies and resulted in participants successfully applying these skills in their research and professional projects.

Advanced Programming Workshop

This workshop focuses on advanced programming techniques, covering topics such as parallel computing, algorithm optimization, and software architecture. Participants will engage in hands-on projects, applying these skills to solve complex problems. Previous workshops have included collaborations with industry experts and resulted in participants developing innovative software solutions.

Seminars & Guest Lectures

Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare

Seminars and guest lectures provide platforms for experts to share insights and research findings on cutting-edge topics. This lecture by Dr. Anya Sharma will explore the transformative applications of AI in healthcare, including diagnostics, treatment planning, and patient care. Attendees will learn about the latest advancements, ethical considerations, and future trends in this rapidly evolving field. Previous lectures have featured renowned researchers and practitioners, fostering discussions that have led to collaborative research opportunities and innovative project ideas.

Cybersecurity Trends and Challenges

A seminar on the latest trends and challenges in cybersecurity, featuring insights from industry leaders and experts. Topics will include emerging threats, defense strategies, and the future of cybersecurity. Attendees will gain valuable knowledge to enhance their understanding and skills in this critical field.

Medical Camps

Free Health Checkup Camp

Medical camps offer essential health services and awareness programs to the community. This free health checkup camp will provide comprehensive health screenings, including blood pressure, blood sugar, and cholesterol checks, along with consultations with healthcare professionals. The camp aims to promote preventive healthcare and early detection of health issues among students and staff. Past camps have served hundreds of individuals, providing crucial health information and referrals for further care.

Eye Care Camp

An eye care camp offering free eye exams, vision screenings, and consultations with ophthalmologists. The camp will also provide information on eye health and preventive measures. This initiative aims to address common vision problems and promote eye care awareness within the community.

Convocation / Annual Day

Annual Day Celebrations

Convocation and Annual Day events celebrate academic achievements and institutional milestones. The Annual Day Celebrations will feature cultural performances by students, recognition of outstanding faculty and students, and a keynote address by a distinguished alumnus. This event fosters a sense of community and pride among students, faculty, and alumni. Previous celebrations have included captivating performances, inspiring speeches, and memorable moments of recognition, strengthening the bonds within the university community.

Graduation Ceremony

The annual graduation ceremony honoring the graduating class of 2024. The event will include speeches from university officials, faculty, and student representatives, along with the presentation of degrees. This ceremony marks the culmination of students' academic journeys and celebrates their achievements.

General News & Events

Campus Sustainability Initiative

An initiative to promote sustainability on campus, including recycling programs, energy conservation measures, and environmental awareness campaigns. Students, faculty, and staff are encouraged to participate and contribute to a greener campus.

Career Fair 2024

The annual career fair connecting students with potential employers from various industries. The event will feature company presentations, networking opportunities, and on-site interviews. Students are encouraged to attend and explore career prospects.
